Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Is Luminary Atelier free to use?

Yes — the gallery, artist profiles, all learning content, and the creative Studio are completely free. No account required.

Can I use artworks from the gallery?

The artworks featured are historical works in the public domain. The Luminary Atelier design, text, and branding are © 2025 Luminary Atelier.

How does the Studio work?

The Studio is a full browser-based painting application with 22+ tools, layers, symmetry, and export. No download or account needed — just open and paint.

Can I save my Studio artwork?

Yes — click the 💾 button or press Ctrl+S to download your artwork as a PNG. JPEG export is also available in the File menu.

How do I suggest an artist or artwork?

We'd love your suggestions! Send us a message using the contact form above with your recommendation and a brief note on why you think it belongs in the collection.

Do you offer educational partnerships?

Yes — we work with schools, universities, and cultural institutions. Select "Education Partnership" in the contact form above and we'll be in touch.
