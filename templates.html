Pro Tips

How to Use Templates

🖌

Start with the Sketch

Use the Pencil tool at low opacity to rough in your composition before committing with colour. Templates provide structure — you provide the vision.

🎨

Change the Palette

Every template is designed with a neutral palette. The real fun starts when you replace the default colours with your own — try the Colour panel's Harmony section.

📚

Study the Masters First

Open the corresponding artist's page in the Gallery before painting. Understanding the style you're working in produces far richer results.

⚡

Use Layers Freely

Don't paint everything on one layer. Use separate layers for background, mid-tones, and detail. You can always merge them when you're happy.

💾

Save Early, Save Often

Press Ctrl+S any time to download your work in progress. Keep multiple saves — so you can always return to a previous stage.

🌐

Share to Community

When you're done, submit your finished template interpretation to the Community Gallery. We'd love to see what you make of each starting point.
